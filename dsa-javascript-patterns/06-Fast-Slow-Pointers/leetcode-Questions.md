⚡ Smart Practice Strategy

1. **Slow & Fast Ratio**: Usually, slow moves 1 step and fast moves 2 steps.
2. **Cycle Termination**: If fast or fast.next becomes null, there's no cycle.
3. **Finding Start**: If a cycle exists, reset slow to head and move both 1 step at a time to find the start of the cycle.
